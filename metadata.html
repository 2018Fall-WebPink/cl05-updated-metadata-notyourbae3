The many positives of coffee

Coffee has many benefits and it is also super delicious! I love it because you can flavor coffee just as you like, perfect for your taste buds! Coffee is a must have in the morning, and it gives you that boost of energy you need!

Coffee health benefits: Coffee may protect against type 2 diabetes, Parkinson's disease, liver disease, liver cancer, and it promotes a healthy heart. Black coffee everyday reduces the risk of cardiovascular diseases including stroke. Black coffee can also reduce the inflammation level in the body

5 Most popular types of coffee: Americano, is simply just hot water to a shot of espresso coffee. Latte, which consists of steamed (or scolded) milk and a single shot of coffee. Cappuccino, is a shot of espresso, then a shot of steamed milk, and finally a layer of frothed, foamy milk. Espresso, boiling water under high pressure through finely ground up coffee beans. Last but definitely not least, is a Flat White, steamed milk is poured over a shot of espresso.
